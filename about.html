Welcome!

Our team was established in August 2017 at Westfield High School by eight FIRST alumni members with the assistance of our school faculty.

Currently, our team is made of 23 students. We also have 8 mentors. We have grown a lot since our rookie year and we are very exited to make our comeback as a veteran team.

As a team of 23, we each had integral roles in both the robot and culture sides of the team. We chose a culture project that we worked on with a mentor each week. We split up the work of designing, programming, building, and wiring the robot. Even though we were all ultimately responsible for our culture project, we still each helped each other. We are fluid in our structure to allow all of us to learn as much as possible.
